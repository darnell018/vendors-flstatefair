Vendor Guide

Rules & Policies

The Florida State Fair prides itself on being the top place for family fun and entertainment every February and could not do it without the dedication of our vendors. These rules and policies are in place to maintain consistant standards throughout our event.

Credentials

NEW for 2023! Online crediential ordering is coming soon to the Florida State Fair. It is required of all vendors to wear credentials at all times when working and when moving throughout the property. Our credentials office will open in mid-January for 2023.

Permits & Parking

All vendor vehicles and ATVs on the innergrounds during the Florida State Fair are required to have a parking pass, stock truck pass, or golf cart permit and must be purchased through our credentials office. Vehicles or ATVs without permits will be towed at the owners expense. Permits will be available for pre-order online in January.
